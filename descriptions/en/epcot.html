Epcot stands for Experimental Prototype Community of Tomorrow and is one out of 4 main parks in Walt Disney World Orlando.
The park is divided into 2 main areas - Future World and World Showcase.

Future World is the place where the main rides are, it's best to plan your route from there since the other area usually opens at 11am.
In Future World you will find some "not to miss" rides such a Soarin where you will sky high over Califronia and Test Track where you get into a car you build yourself while testing it on extreme conditions.

World Showcase provide the guests with experiences from different countries all over the world - Japan, China, Mexico, France, Canada & more.
