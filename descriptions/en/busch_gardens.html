Busch Gardnes is part of SeaWorld Parks and is located in Tampa, Florida.
It's a special combination between a zoo and a theme park, all african style.

Busch Gardens offers great and very extreme rides including falling 90 degress down while riding a rollercoaster.
You will be able to find lots of different species of animals in the park. You can take a ride to see it all from close or from up above using the rides in the parks.

It's important to take into account that getting to Busch Gardens may take about an hour from Orlando since it's located in Tamps.
