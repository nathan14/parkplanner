Island of Advantures is one of two parks in Universal Stuidos Orlando Resort.
The park is divided into different section - Marvel's Superheo Land, Wizarding World of Harry Potter and more..
The rides in Island of Advantures considered to be "very extreme" so you won't see too many little children around this park.
