Sea World in Orlando, Florida is part of Sea World Parks group.
It's a theme park combined with a zoo which specifies in Marine animals.
Tha main attractions in Sea World are the shows - the best and most famous shows include Whales, Dolphins and Sea Lions.
Other than shows, the park offers some great rides such as Manta & The Kraken.
While touring the park, you will be able to see white whales, polar bears, fish, penguins and many more!
