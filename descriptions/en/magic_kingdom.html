Magic Kingdom Park is the main park out of 4 parks in Walt Disney World Orlando, Florida.
The park is divided into a few "lands" with different themes.
Those who visited Disneyland Paris, might find many areas familiar.

Each land will provide different expirence and going through the park and seeing all land is a must.
An area where lots of change occured recently, is the New Fantayland where you can meet Bell (Beauty & The Beast), Gaston and many more...
You will get the opportunity to dine at The Beast's castle and ride the Seven Dwarfs new rollecoaster.
